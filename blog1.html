Building ESG Capacity for Smallholder Farmers

Smallholder farmers are at the heart of agricultural economies in Africa. However, they often lack the tools, training, and resources needed to meet ESG standards. Building their capacity involves practical training, inclusive governance, and access to certification support services. At Bluesky ESG Solutions Ltd., we work closely with producers to bridge these gaps and enable access to sustainable markets.
